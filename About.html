Welcome to the Metrology Lab

Precision. Innovation. Workforce Excellence.
Our mission: to lead in materials measurement, semiconductor metrology, and hands‑on skill development for the next generation of engineers and technicians.

Lab Overview

Our Metrology Lab supports advanced measurement and materials characterization — from semiconductor wafers, thin‑films, and surface topography to elemental composition and defect analysis. Equipped with state‑of‑the‑art instruments such as scanning electron microscopes (SEM), atomic force microscopes (AFM), profilometers, EDX analyzers, and thin‑film measurement tools — we provide R&D‑level analysis, quality‑control services, and workforce development. We also run training programs, apprenticeships, and educational outreach to cultivate skilled metrology professionals familiar with calibration, data analysis, and measurement standards.

Facilities & Rooms

Instrument Room

High‑precision instruments (SEM, AFM, profilometer, thin‑film measurement rigs) for surface & materials characterization.

Sample Prep Lab

Polishing stations, sputter coaters, clean benches — prepare wafers, metal samples, coatings, and more with utmost precision.

Training & Development Room

Dedicated training area for workforce development: microscopy workshops, calibration exercises, and student‑focused hands‑on sessions.
