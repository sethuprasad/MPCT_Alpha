Keyence VHX-7000 4K Digital Microscope — Equipment Overview
The Keyence VHX-7000 is a state-of-the-art 4K optical microscope widely used in semiconductor fabs, R&D labs, failure analysis, and advanced material characterization. Known for its ultra-high accuracy, deep depth-of-field, and automated measurement workflows, the VHX-7000 allows researchers to inspect, quantify, and document microscopic features with unmatched clarity.

Core Features

4K Ultra-High-Resolution Imaging
Advanced Auto-Focus & HDR Enhancement
Multi-Angle Observation & Tilt Stages
Automated Measurement Tools
3D Surface Profiling Mode
Non-Contact Height & Roughness Analysis
Rapid Image Stitching (Up to 50,000 pixels wide)
Built-in Report Generator for Documentation

Applications

Semiconductor Defect Inspection
Bond Pad & Metallization Analysis
MEMS Structure Inspection
Crack & Fracture Characterization
Surface Roughness Studies
Composite Material Analysis
