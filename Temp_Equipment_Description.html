Keyence VHX-7000 Digital Microscope

The Keyence VHX-7000 is an advanced all-in-one optical microscope offering ultra-high-resolution imaging, automatic depth composition, multi-angle illumination, and precise dimensional measurements. It is widely used in semiconductor inspection, wafer surface characterization, failure analysis, micro-structure evaluation, and materials science.

How This Equipment is Used in the Lab

  1. Sample Placement: The wafer, die, metal piece, or material sample is positioned on the motorized stage.
  2. Auto-Focus & Depth Composition: The system automatically sets focal layers to create a fully focused image.
  3. Select Illumination Mode: Choose from brightfield, darkfield, polarized, or mixed lighting for defects.
  4. Measurement & Analysis: Features like cracks, voids, grain boundaries, particles, and scratches are measured.
  5. 3D Surface Profiling: Height differences, roughness, and step heights are captured.
  6. Final Report Generation: The system auto-creates a branded PDF report with annotations.

Safety & Handling Notes

  • Do not apply pressure on lenses or camera units.
  • Ensure samples are clean before placing on the stage.
  • Follow ESD protocols for semiconductor wafers.
  • Shut down the illumination system after sessions to prevent LED aging.
  • Always log your usage time for traceability.
